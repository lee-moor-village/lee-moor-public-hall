Facilities

The hall has some great facilities available.

Food and Drink

We have a proper kitchen and well stocked bar. The bar tends to be open most Wednesday nights with food available to order. If you are booking the hall for your own event, we may be able to help with the catering.

Stage

Audio, lighting equipment etc.

Games

Skittles, pool, darts, bingo, quiz nights.
